About Anthony Ebo Spio

My goal is to research, teach and apply the insights of business, entrepreneurship and marketing to help start and develop enterprises and create jobs and wealth in Africa. Consequently, I am committed to a career in teaching, research and practice of marketing, marketing communication, enterprise development, business strategy, and branding and local economic development. I also have interest in international development projects and interventions to alleviate poverty and promote development in developing countries
